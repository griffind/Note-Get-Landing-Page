noteGet is a democratic forum for university students

Students contribute notes, study guides, papers, and other resources to specific departments and classes within their university.

Students can also post to their university's general forum for discussion about things going on around campus, or anything else they think their peers would like.

Like many of your favorite websites, the best content is pushed to the top

Notes for a particular class, exam content, and material discussion all get voted on, so you can expect to know what you will be graded on.

No more surprises!
